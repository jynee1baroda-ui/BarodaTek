┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ROLLBACK PROCEDURE                                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  If deployment breaks something:
  
  Option 1: Restore from backup
  ──────────────────────────────
  
  $latest = Get-ChildItem ..\barodatek_backup_* | 
            Sort-Object LastWriteTime -Descending | 
            Select-Object -First 1
  
  Copy-Item $latest\* . -Recurse -Force
  vercel --prod --yes
  
  
  Option 2: Rollback via Vercel
  ──────────────────────────────
  
  vercel ls --prod                    ◄─── List deployments
  vercel rollback <previous-url>      ◄─── Rollback to working version
