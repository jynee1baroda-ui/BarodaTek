└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  After deployment:

  1. Wait 30-60 seconds for CDN
  
  2. Visit: https://barodatek.com
  
  3. Hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R
  
  4. Check:

     □ Logo in navbar
     □ Profile photo in About
     □ All sections work
     □ No console errors

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING                                            │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Issue: Old content on live site
  Fix: Wait 5 min, then Ctrl+Shift+R
       OR force redeploy

  Issue: Port 8080 in use
  Fix: netstat -ano | findstr :8080
       taskkill /PID <PID> /F

  Issue: Black screen
  Fix: Restore from backup:
       Copy-Item ..\barodatek_backup_*\* . -Force
       vercel --prod --yes

  Issue: Missing files
  Fix: node scripts/prepare-deploy.js
       (Shows what's missing)
